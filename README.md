> A curated list of resources, databases, and official sources on drone laws, regulations, and UAV/UAS policies around the world.

Understanding drone laws is essential for safe and legal operations, especially for hobbyists, professionals, and researchers flying across borders. This list compiles reliable country-specific legal resources, licensing guides, and aviation authority links.

## Global Aggregators

- [UAV Coach Drone Laws Database](https://uavcoach.com/drone-laws/) – Easy-to-read summaries of drone laws by country.
- [Drone Laws by Country (DroneTraveler)](https://www.dronetraveller.com/) – Visual guide with regulations, travel restrictions, and more.
- [DJI FlySafe GEO Zone Map](https://www.dji.com/flysafe/geo-map) – Flight restrictions by region for DJI users.
- [ICAO – UAS Toolkit](https://www.icao.int/safety/UA/Pages/default.aspx) – International Civil Aviation Organization’s drone regulation resources.
- [Wikipedia – UAV Regulations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulation_of_unmanned_aerial_vehicles) – Summary of legal status by country.

## Africa

- **Kenya** – [Kenya Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA)](https://www.kcaa.or.ke/) – Drone licensing and regulations.
- **South Africa** – [South African Civil Aviation Authority](https://www.caa.co.za/) – Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems (RPAS) framework.
- **Nigeria** – [Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority](https://ncaa.gov.ng/) – UAS licensing and approval information.

## Asia

- **India** – [DigitalSky Platform](https://digitalsky.dgca.gov.in/) – Drone registration and permissions.
- **China** – [Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)](http://www.caac.gov.cn/) – UAV policies and airspace restrictions.
- **Japan** – [Japan Civil Aviation Bureau (JCAB)](https://www.mlit.go.jp/en/koku/drone.html) – Flying zones, permits, and penalties.
- **Singapore** – [Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS)](https://www.caas.gov.sg/) – Requirements for recreational and commercial use.

## Europe

- **European Union (EASA)** – [European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA)](https://www.easa.europa.eu/domains/civil-drones-rpas) – Unified rules for EU member states.
- **United Kingdom** – [UK Civil Aviation Authority (CAA)](https://www.caa.co.uk/drones/) – Registration and operator ID requirements.
- **Germany** – [Luftfahrt-Bundesamt (LBA)](https://www.lba.de/) – National implementation of EU drone laws.
- **France** – [Direction Générale de l'Aviation Civile (DGAC)](https://www.ecologie.gouv.fr/drones-loisir-et-professionnels) – French drone laws.
- **Italy** – [ENAC – Italian Civil Aviation Authority](https://www.enac.gov.it/) – Licensing and no-fly zones.

## North America

- **United States** – [FAA – UAS Regulations](https://www.faa.gov/uas) – Registration, Remote ID, airspace rules.
- **Canada** – [Transport Canada Drone Safety](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/drone-safety) – Drone pilot certificate and safety guidelines.
- **Mexico** – [Agencia Federal de Aviación Civil (AFAC)](https://www.gob.mx/afac) – UAV policies and weight classifications.

## Oceania

- **Australia** – [Civil Aviation Safety Authority (CASA)](https://www.casa.gov.au/drones) – Drone rules and safety apps.
- **New Zealand** – [Civil Aviation Authority of New Zealand](https://www.aviation.govt.nz/drones/) – Recreational and commercial drone rules.

## South America

- **Brazil** – [ANAC – Agência Nacional de Aviação Civil](https://www.anac.gov.br/) – Drone registration and operating guidelines.
- **Argentina** – [Administración Nacional de Aviación Civil (ANAC)](https://www.argentina.gob.ar/anac) – UAV use and registration laws.
- **Colombia** – [Unidad Administrativa Especial de Aeronáutica Civil](https://www.aerocivil.gov.co/) – Drone use for photography and mapping.

## Additional Resources

- [No Fly Drones – UK Map](https://www.noflydrones.co.uk/) – Visual no-fly zones in the UK.
- [Airmap](https://airmap.com/) – Airspace maps and drone flight planning tools.
- [OpenSky Network](https://opensky-network.org/) – Real-time air traffic data and airspace surveillance.
